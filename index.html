      Our Story

      Welcome to the culinary world of Piyush Saraf, where tradition meets taste. Our cafe is a one-stop destination for food lovers who crave authenticity and variety. We take pride in serving a wide array of flavors, ranging from the comforting crunch of South Indian classics and Traditional Indian meals to refreshing Fresh Juices and healthy breakfast options. We understand the value of a home-cooked meal, which is why our dedicated Tiffin Centre provides nutritious and delicious food for those living away from home. For the street-food lovers, our Pani Puri stall offers that perfect, tangy burst of flavor, prepared with the highest standards of hygiene and love. Founded and managed by Piyush Saraf, our mission is to provide high-quality, flavorful food that satisfies every palate—whether you're stopping by for a quick snack, a healthy juice, or a full traditional meal. Taste the tradition, feel the warmth.
